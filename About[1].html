Our Mission

The cornerstone and legacy of the Hardin County High School Band program is striving for excellence through dedication, learning, experience, and a team effort from students, boosters, and staff.

Student Involvement

Your student can stay involved in this band program in many ways. Your student can participate in various bands and ensembles such as the Varsity Band, Full Marching Band, Color Guard, Pep Band, Patriot Pride Ensemble, Christmas Band, Jazz Band, Percussion Ensembles, Tuba Christmas, Concert Band, and Wind Ensemble.

Parent Involvement

Parents can stay involved by helping with the Band Boosters you can help by working in the concession stand, chaperoning band trips, and helping with any other fundraisers.

P1, P2, P3, P4 Process

P1 = You made an 80 or above on your marching and instrumental audition and made a high enough chair to qualify for varsity band. You are required to attend all varsity rehearsals and preformances.

P2 = You can an 80 or above on your marching and instrumental audition but you did not make a high enough chair to qualify for varsity band. However, your position gives you the opportunity to challenge any P1 for a spot in the show. You are not required to attend varsity rehearsals, however, it is highly recomended that you attend if you plan on challenging for a spot in the show. You are still required to attend all games and full band preformances.

P3 = You did not score an 80 or above on you marching and instrumental audition and do not qualify for the varsity band. You will not have the oppurtunity to challenge until you have passed your marching and instrumental audition. You will still be required to attend all games and full band rehearsals. Once you passed your marching and instrumental audition, you will move up to a P2 classifiaction.

P4 = You are injured or you have another medical excuse that will prevent you from preforming. You will not be allowed to preform until the director receives a written release from your doctor.
